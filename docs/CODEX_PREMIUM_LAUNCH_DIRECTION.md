# CROW & CROWN — Premium Launch Direction

Date: 2026-06-19  
Supersedes plain bundle language from the first handoff.

## Why this revision exists

The first launch handoff was functional but too generic. `Mega Bundle` and `Basics Pack` read like commodity marketplace language. The storefront should feel more boutique, curated, and premium.

## New product architecture

Use a premium ladder instead of a discount-bundle ladder.

### Hero tier

The complete product should feel like the collector item, not a file dump.

- `THE ICON EDIT — Complete Exhibition Archive` — $89
- `THE COURT IS MY CANVAS — Complete Art Court Archive` — $79

### À la carte tiers

These let shoppers buy smaller packages without making the brand feel cheap.

- `Invitation Atelier`
- `Signage Gallery`
- `Favor & Label Archive`
- `Invitation Set`
- `Signage Set`
- `Favor & Label Set`

### Printed-for-you tier

Physical Printful products should feel like gallery pieces, not generic posters.

- `Welcome Sign, Printed for You`
- `Gallery Welcome Sign, Printed for You`
- `Photo Booth Backdrop Print`
- `Court Backdrop Print`

## Naming rules

Use:
- Archive
- Atelier
- Gallery
- Suite
- Edition
- Printed for You
- Ready-to-Print Collection

Avoid:
- Mega Bundle
- Basics Pack
- Starter Pack
- Huge Bundle
- Cheap
- Value Pack
- All-in-one dump

## Storefront direction

Homepage hierarchy:

1. Hero: `THE ICON EDIT — Complete Exhibition Archive`
2. Secondary row: `Invitation Atelier`, `Signage Gallery`, `Favor & Label Archive`
3. Printed-for-you row: welcome sign, gallery sign, backdrop print
4. Separate lower collection block: `THE COURT IS MY CANVAS`

Do not show every product at once above the fold. Premium means fewer choices, stronger spacing, clearer hierarchy, and better naming.

## Product page copy direction

Use restrained editorial copy.

Example:

```markdown
A complete ready-to-print party archive designed like a children’s fashion exhibition.

THE ICON EDIT brings together the invitation moment, gallery signage, table details, favor packaging, and print guidance in one polished digital collection.

No personalization. No template editing. Just print, trim, style, and install.
```

Avoid over-explaining. Avoid marketplace-style keyword stuffing in the visible copy. Put SEO keywords in backend SEO fields and tags.

## Pricing direction

Digital:

| Tier | Target price |
|---|---:|
| Complete Exhibition Archive | $89 |
| Complete Art Court Archive | $79 |
| Invitation Atelier / Invitation Set | $24–$28 |
| Signage Gallery / Signage Set | $30–$34 |
| Favor & Label Archive / Set | $24–$26 |

Physical Printful:

| Product | Price floor |
|---|---:|
| 18x24 printed sign | $49 |
| 24x36 printed sign | $69 |
| 36x24 printed backdrop/poster | $79 |

Codex must still calculate final Printful pricing from live product cost and shipping. Do not publish physical products below the price floor without explicit approval.

## QA additions

Add one extra QA gate:

> No commodity/cheap product naming may appear on the live storefront.

The store should feel like a boutique printable collection, not a bulk download shop.
